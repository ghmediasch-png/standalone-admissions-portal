About GH Media School

GH Media School is a Media Training institution approved by the GHANA EDUCATION SERVICE (GES) and Commission For Technical and Vocational Education and Training (CTVET). It is the first to start under the GH Brand with the objective of creating a reputable hub to train and equip talents with the necessary skill set to meet the competitive demands of the media and film making industry. Courses offered Television and Film Production, Multimedia Production, Journalism and Media Studies, Journalism and Media Arts.

GH Media School prides itself in working closely with most giant media outlets (TV and Radio) including UTV, GH One, TV Africa, Metro TV, Atinka TV amongst others and has attracted the presence of renowned media personalities like MAJID MICHEL, TIMOTHY BENTUM, ABEKU SANTANA, JOHN DUMELO ETC. Our practical-based orientation has attracted a significant student population of about 5000 and still counting and has made our graduates very relevant and well equipped in their chosen field of study.

Entry Requirements

Skills Acquisition Pathway

  • Any grade from JHS, SSSCE / WASSCE
  • Must have at least one year of relevant working experience
  • No results or No qualifications are required

Academic Progression Pathway

A. CTVET DIPLOMA

  • Five (5) SSCE/WASSCE including passes in English and Maths/GBCE Passes
  • Five (5) old Certificate II Passes including English Language and Mathematics
  • Five (5) New Certificate II passes including English Language and Mathematics
  • One Year Certificate Programme in a relevant area from any accredited institution

B. CTVET CERTIFICATE

  • Four (4) SSCE/WASSCE passes including English Language and Mathematics/GBCE passes
  • Four (4) Old Certificate II passes including English Language and Mathematics
  • Four (4) New Certificate passes including English Language and Mathematics

OR

  • Must be at least 25 years old and must show proof of date of birth with national document
  • Must have at least two (2) years relevant working experience

Note: Students who complete our 2 years CTVET diploma can gain admissions into the second year in a technical university in Ghana

Duration and Certificate Awarded

  • CERTIFICATE OF COMPETENCE - 1 YEAR
  • ADVANCE CERTIFICATE OF COMPETENCE - 2 YEARS
  • CTVET DIPLOMA CERTIFICATE - 2 YEARS
